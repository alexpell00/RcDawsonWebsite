About The Team

We are a high school robotics team located in Lafayette, Colorado and are part of the Alexander Dawson community. RC Dawson, or Team 2972, gives students a way to pursue an interest in science, technology, engineering, and math. RC Dawson is made possible by our exceptional sponsors and mentors, without which the team would not be as strong.

RC Dawson started in 2008 by Ryan Depew, the primary Physics teacher at the time. The team was originally called "Varsity Physics" but the name was later changed to "Robotics Club Dawson" or "RC Dawson" for short.

During the 2011 competition, RC Dawson qualified for the national FRC championships. This robot's success would create the new standard for RC Dawson's success in upcoming years. Most recently the team scored third and fifth for points scored in the Autonomous and Tele-op periods, respectively.
